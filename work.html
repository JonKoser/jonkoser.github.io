Wildland Urban Interface - Minnesota

This is one of the spreads I helped produce throught he UW-Cartography Lab for the National Forest Service. It is one of 49 such spreads, one or each conterminous US state plus Washington D.C, created for an atlas showing the Wildland Urban Interface for forest fires.

Full Sized Image

May I Get You a Drink?

This is a map I made as a project for school. It shows the change in access to drinking water in African countries between the years 2002 and 2008. It was made with ArcGIS and Adobe Illustrator.

Full Sized Image

Old Salt Route

This is my map of the Old Salt Route in northern Germany. This map won the CaGIS Arthur Robinson Award in cartography as well as the Barbar Petchenik award for cartographic design in 2014 and is now archived in the Library of Congress.
